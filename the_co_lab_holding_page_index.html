Building resilient and inclusive local economies

We work with communities, governments and partners to catalyse local action across diverse contexts. We connect changemakers, resources and ideas to deliver place‑based transformation that is just, scalable and lasting.

Partner with us Email us

What we focus on

  • Place‑based chapters to coordinate local action
  • Enterprise incubation and economic diversification
  • Partnering and aligning finance for impact (incl. IPP ED)
  • Learning by doing: open playbooks and practice notes

Why we exist

In many places, significant income and resources flow through the local economy, yet benefits concentrate among a few and inequalities grow. Old models that appoint leaders and build structures first can empower gatekeeping rather than action.

We take a stepping‑into‑action, partnership‑of‑the‑willing approach that follows energy, surfaces real changemakers and avoids destructive infighting. Immersion helps us understand dynamics, build trust and back doers.

How we work

  • Participant observation and immersion
  • Action research (learning by doing)
  • Partnering Implementation Model (EDP)
  • Open calls, microgrants and light engine rooms

We support the just transition by linking decarbonisation with decent work, SMME growth and spatial justice.

Municipal and public partners

Co‑design chapters aligned to local strategies; aggregate pipelines; build delivery capacity and transparent dashboards.

Corporate and IPP ED

Align enterprise development and social investment to place‑based portfolios. Leverage ED budgets and blended finance.

Donors and ecosystems

Back immersive action research, microgrants and incubation. Share learning openly to influence practice.
